About Me

What is there to say? Perhaps, I could say who I am, what I do and my reason for being here. For doing this thing. But why is something hard for me to figure out right now. See, I am a creative individual, I am a writer and a puzzle maker of sorts.It all started with the creation of a few short stories and only after twenty years did I figure out that there is a career in the real world where I can merge the critical thought process of solving puzzles and using imagination to bring things to life. So, after all these years, here I am. I am learning tools to build things. Using html, css, javascript and the multitude of other developer tools I am able to make things happen, to make things beautiful. So that is my mission and that is my why.
